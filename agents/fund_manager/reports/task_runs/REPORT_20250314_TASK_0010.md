## Market Context Analysis

**Observation:** Mean reversion outperformed momentum in this 2-year period.

**Possible Explanations:**
1. Market was more range-bound than trending
2. Short-term reversals more common than sustained trends
3. High volatility created mean-reversion opportunities

**2023-2024 Market Environment:**
- Post-pandemic recovery
- Fed rate hikes and pauses
- AI-driven tech rallies (intermittent)
- Overall choppy market conditions

## Conclusions

### Strategic Implications

1. **Market Regime Matters**
   - Mean reversion works better in choppy/range markets
   - Momentum works better in trending markets

2. **Risk-Adjusted Returns**
   - Mean reversion provided better Sharpe ratio
   - Lower drawdown with similar returns

3. **Trade Frequency**
   - Momentum generates more signals
   - Mean reversion more selective but higher quality

### Recommendations

| Scenario | Suggested Strategy |
|----------|-------------------|
| Strong trending market | Momentum |
| Range-bound/choppy market | Mean Reversion |
